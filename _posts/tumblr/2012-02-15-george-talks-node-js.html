Our Creative Director, George (@shapeshed), took to the stage on Monday night to give a talk on Node.js. The event was hosted by Forward who gave us free beer and pizza - thank you Forward!

An Introduction to Node.js from London Web Standards on Vimeo.

The evening was a great success - as we heard from numerous people afterwards - and it was great to hear from a few companies that are already creating successful applications using Node.js.

George is also writing a book on the subject, titled ’Sam’s Teach Yourself Node.js in 24 Hours’. It is due for release later this year and you can pre-order on Amazon.

For more information and to get involved, check out ’lnug’ (London Node User Group), also hosted by Forward - next meetup next Wednesday 22nd February! (as we were told just a few times).
