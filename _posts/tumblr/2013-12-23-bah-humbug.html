“Every idiot who goes about with ‘Merry Christmas’ on his lips, should be boiled with his own pudding, and buried with a stake of holly through his heart.” -Ebenezer Scrooge

Whilst most of my pebble comrades were spreading Christmas cheer with hugs, interactive murals, and happy Santas I decided to take a slightly less merry approach.

Fed up with christmas invading our lives for more and more time each year, I created Zero Christmas a simple bookmarklet that lets you censor any mention of christmas from a web page. I borrowed (stole) heavily from another pebble hack I had created for the Olympics hack day, Fear the IOC. I refactored the code a little, added a 'naughty list’ of christmas words, and detection and destruction of christmas colours in css. See below for an example.

I also tried to implement color-thief to detect christmasy images automatically. Alas the time restraints of the hack-day defeated me and I didn’t get this working.

Try Zero Christmas for yourself here and let us know what you think in the comments.

Bah! humbug!
